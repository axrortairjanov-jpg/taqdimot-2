Founded 2016, Tashkent

We got tired of itineraries that read like spreadsheets.

Wayfare started as three friends rewriting their own bad vacations. Ten years later, we plan trips for people who want the same thing we did: fewer boxes ticked, more mornings that matter.

How we got here

2016

Three friends, one bad trip

A rigid 12-stop tour of Italy convinced us there had to be a better way to travel.

2019

First 1,000 travelers

Word of mouth carried us past our first thousand bookings, mostly through friends of friends.

2022

Local partners in 40 cities

We stopped building routes from guidebooks and started building them with people who live there.

2026

15,000+ trips planned

Today, across 120 destinations — still answering booking emails ourselves.

What we won't compromise on

Pace over packing

A full itinerary isn't a good one. We leave room on purpose.

People, not platforms

Every route is checked by someone who's walked it, not just mapped it.

One honest price

What you see at booking is what you pay on arrival. No exceptions.
